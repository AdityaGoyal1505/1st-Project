Dr. Dinesh Goyal

Dr. Dinesh Goyal, is currently working as Principal and Professor in Computer science and Engineering at Poornima Institute of Engineering & Technology. He has more than 23 years of experience in Research and Academic Activities. He specializes in NAAC, NBA, NIRF and other Accreditation and is empanelled NAAC Assessor. His past experience includes Dean Academics, Head of Department and Chief proctor too. His expertise in Information Security, Image Processing, Data Analytics and Cloud Computing. He has research and development funding of Rs. 81 lac on his name and has published 90+ SCI/Scopus/ESCI indexed papers. He has guided 21 PhD Scholars as Supervisor/Co-supervisor, more than 20 PG Research Scholars and guiding more than 6 Ph.D. Research Scholars. He also has 20+ patents published with him
